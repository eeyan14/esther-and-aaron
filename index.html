Our Story

The two met over spring break, and Aaron judged Esther for doing homework :( but he also thought she was cute, so it's okay. Turns out they had a class together (fate???) but the thought of a relationship hadn't crossed Aaron's mind (although it did cross Esther's). Fast-forward a couple of months, and Aaron finally got the hint that Esther was interested in him (one 11-hour study session in Fireside + two lunch dates later...). He asked her out in May (used bookstore scavenger hunt!), but less than a week later, they left for opposite ends of the country for their internships. Fortunately, they discovered that conversation was easy and often, and the relationship took off from there.

They realized through traversing the stresses of senior design projects, a second stretch of long distance, and graduate school (0/10 would not recommend this combination), that life would just be more fun together. On December 26, 2018, Aaron got down on one knee and "surprised" Esther (she had been nagging him for longer than she would like to admit) and of course she said yes! And the rest, as they say, is history.
